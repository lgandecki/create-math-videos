# Lesson 4 — Percent Growth • "Phone Battery Drain"

## Exercise Flow

Your phone is at **80 %**. It loses **25 %** of what it has every hour while gaming.  
Where will it be after the first hour?

Percent decay is multiplicative, so we move to the **log scales**.

```ts
setExpectedBatteryLevel(40);
```

### Check

```js
waitForBatteryLevel(40);
```

#### Incorrect Answer

Not quite yet..

#### Correct Answer

Good job! You’ve correctly set the battery level!
